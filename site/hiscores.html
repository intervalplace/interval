The Sixteen

One race per skill, and each one can only be won once. A race that is over names its winner for as long as the world lasts. A race still running names whoever is closest, and how much of the climb they have behind them. The last level alone is 1,112,977 experience, near a seventh of the whole ascent, so a citizen can sit in sight of mastery for a very long time while everyone watches.
